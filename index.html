Who We Are

Since 2018, the Queer Business Alliance has championed LGBTQ+ individuals through the creation of collaborative events and experiences designed to foster connection and advance LGBTQ+ professionals.

Our events and mentorship programs elevate our members by providing a context for authentic dialogue between LGBTQ+ professionals and access to a network of highly-driven business owners who are eager to connect with others in the LGBTQ+ community.

Why Become a Member? 

 

We are comprised of real LGBTQ+ business owners, who are passionate about seeing LGBTQ+ people thrive.

We believe in doing this by equipping one another with tools and resources to be successful in our businesses (and yes, that means financially successful)

Every LGBTQ+ biz owner is welcome — CEO’s, founders, solopreneurs — wherever you come from and whatever you do, there is a place for you here.

Serve on a Committee

The most direct way to get your voice heard is by volunteering on a committee. Committees are responsible for shaping the QBA. Every monthly board meeting includes a report from each committee’s representative. Committees include: Membership, Mentorship, Events, Communication & Tech, and Fundraising.

Apply to Speak or Perform at an Event

 Inspiration, Mentorship, and Leadership are 3 main pillars of QBA. We strive to include LGBTQ+ speakers, performers, and artists at our events. If you’re interested in taking the stage at a QBA event, this might be a good volunteer opportunity for you.

Sponsorship Opportunities

Corporate Partners and Event Sponsors keep the QBA running. Donate and engage with Sponsorship Opportunities to help us amplify LGBTQ+ voices + experiences in biz.
